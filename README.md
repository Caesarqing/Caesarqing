## My Cyber Philosophy

**Explore:** Curiosity is the fuel. I always want to know what happens if I stitch this thing to that thing.  
**探索：** 好奇心是燃料，永远想知道“如果把这个和那个缝在一起会怎么样？”

**Passion:** Tinkering is a lifestyle. Explosions are just part of the process. Laughing through the chaos is the real output.  
**激情：** 瞎折腾是生活态度，爆炸只是过程，笑出腹肌才是结果。

**Create:** I turn mental tentacles into products, reaching toward more people, more problems, and more possibilities.  
**创造：** 把大脑里的触手伸向现实，用产品去触摸更多人、更多问题、更多可能。

**Endeavor:** I keep moving. The point is not only to reach the destination, but to make the road stranger, freer, and more interesting.  
**努力：** 一直在路上。目标不只是到达终点，而是让这条路越来越有趣、越来越荒诞、越来越自由。

## What I Believe

A product is not a feature list.  
It is not just buttons, pages, dashboards, or carefully arranged boxes pretending to be strategy.

A product is a thinking tentacle reaching into reality.  
It probes problems, senses change, connects people, grabs opportunities, and occasionally touches things it probably should not have touched.

Technology is not a god.  
AI is not a savior.

They are more like newly grown organs —  
letting humans reach farther, think faster, and sometimes tie themselves into a beautiful, expensive knot.

So I see product-making as a way of exploration.  
Not to prove that I am clever,  
but to check whether this chaotic world can still be connected in a slightly better way.

---

产品不是功能列表，  
也不是按钮、页面、仪表盘，或者一堆假装很有战略感的方块。

产品是思维伸向现实世界的触手。  
它替我们试探问题，感知变化，连接人群，抓住机会，偶尔也会摸到一些不该摸的东西。

技术不是神，  
AI 也不是救世主。

它们更像新长出来的器官——  
让人类摸得更远、想得更快，也更容易把自己绕成一团昂贵而优雅的麻花。

所以我更愿意把做产品当成一种探索方式。  
不是为了证明自己多聪明，  
而是为了确认：这个混乱的世界，是否还能被重新连接一次。
